What is Renewable energy?

Key Takeaways

  • Renewable energy comes from natural sources like the sun, wind, and water.
  • Solar power is the fastest-growing energy source globally.
  • Wind energy is one of the most efficient and scalable renewable sources.
  • Hydropower is the largest renewable energy source worldwide.
  • Geothermal energy taps into Earthâ€™s heat for sustainable power.
  • Bioenergy uses organic materials like crops and waste for fuel.
  • Renewable energy significantly reduces greenhouse gas emissions.
  • Unlike fossil fuels, renewable energy sources are not depleted.
  • Advancements in battery storage help store excess renewable energy.
  • Many countries are transitioning to 100% renewable energy for a sustainable future.
